Internships and College Credits

Internships are provided yearly for high school and college graduates at the age of 18 and over.

College students majoring in Art may apply for afternoon/weekend classes to earn college credits.

Learn more

Educational Programs and Trips

Museum staff and teachers from other institutions come to teach kids from pre-k to junior high school.

Twice a year kids with valid membership are invited to participate in a trip with their parents/guardians.

Learn more
